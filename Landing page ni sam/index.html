Our Story

A Legacy of Trailblazers

MSU-MCEST — the Mindanao State University Maigo College of Education, Science and Technology — traces its roots to the Maigo High School, established in 1948 by the community of Maigo, Lanao del Norte.

Originally known as the Maigo School of Arts and Trades (MSAT), the institution was formally integrated into the Mindanao State University System in 2001. The renaming to MSU-MCEST reflects its expanded identity as a center for education, science, and technology.

1948
Maigo High School founded by the community's Parent-Teacher Association.
R.A. 2408
Converted into the Maigo Trade School, expanding vocational programs.
2001
Integrated into the MSU System as Maigo School of Arts and Trades (MSAT).
Present
Rebranded as MSU-MCEST, reflecting full college status and broader academic mandate.

Academic Offerings

Programs Offered

Degree and technical-vocational programs designed to produce globally competitive graduates.

💻

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

BSCS

Builds strong foundations in algorithms, software engineering, data structures, and computing systems for careers in tech and research.

🎒

Bachelor of Elementary Education

BEED

Prepares future elementary school teachers with strong pedagogical skills and subject mastery to nurture young learners effectively.

🔧

Bachelor of Technology and Livelihood Education

BTLED

Combines technical-vocational skills with education methods, preparing teachers for home economics, industrial arts, and livelihood tracks.

⚖️

Bachelor of Science in Criminology

BSCRIM

Covers criminal justice, forensic science, law enforcement, and public safety — preparing graduates for careers in law and order.

🤝

Bachelor of Science in Social Work

BSSW

Trains compassionate professionals to assist individuals, families, and communities in addressing social challenges and promoting well-being.

🍽️

Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management

BSHM

Develops leaders in the hospitality and tourism industry with expertise in hotel operations, food service, and events planning.

🏗️

Bachelor of Industrial Technology

BIT

A hands-on program covering electronics, electrical, mechanical, and drafting technology for industrial and technical careers.

📐

Diploma in Electronics Technology

DET

A focused diploma program equipping students with practical electronics skills for immediate industry employment and technical work.
